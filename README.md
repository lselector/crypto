About Blockchain
<br>
<br>"Blockchain is a system of recording information 
<br>in a way that makes it difficult or impossible 
<br>to change, hack, or cheat the system. 
<br>
<br>A blockchain is essentially a digital ledger of transactions 
<br>that is duplicated and distributed across the entire network 
<br>of computer systems on the blockchain."
<br>
<br>Blockchain is the technology behind crypto-currencies.
<br>As of the end of 2021 there are approx 8,000 crypto-currencies.
