A Thousand Brains: A New Theory of Intelligence, by
Jeff Hawkins. 

Few subjects have captured the imaginations of science
fiction writers like artificial intelligence. If
you’re interested in learning more about what it might
take to create a true AI, this book offers a
fascinating theory. Hawkins may be best known as the
co-inventor of the PalmPilot, but he’s spent decades
thinking about the connections between neuroscience
and machine learning, and there’s no better
introduction to his thinking than this book.
